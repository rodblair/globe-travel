## Design Context

### Users
Primary users are friend groups planning trips together. They use the product to discover destinations, turn ideas into a real itinerary, and collect feedback from friends before booking or committing.

### Brand Personality
The interface should feel easy to understand, confident, and social. It should help a group make decisions without feeling busy or complicated.

### Aesthetic Direction
Use `impeccable.style` as the visual quality bar and design direction: refined, intentional, and editorial rather than flashy. Avoid clutter, stacked gimmicks, and noisy “AI travel app” patterns. Support both light and dark themes over time, but preserve strong contrast as a hard requirement.

### Design Principles
1. Make group planning legible at a glance: hierarchy before decoration.
2. Reduce duplication and visual noise; every panel must earn its space.
3. Keep maps and itinerary tightly connected so movement through the trip feels obvious.
4. Use contrast aggressively enough that controls, routes, and labels are always readable.
5. Make collaboration feel lightweight and calm, not operationally heavy.
